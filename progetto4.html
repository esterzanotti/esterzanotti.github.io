#PROJECT4

#MESCOLIAMOCI

Group project made for Politecnico of Turin-Design per la comunicazione

Melting pot of Turin

Designing a hypothetical municipal communication of Turin means knowing how to tell the story, art, plots, magic, curiosity and diversity that characterize the streets of the city.
The working group that carried out this project is made up of people from different places, with different attitudes, values, interests and habits. Precisely for this reason the concept highlights Turin's most engaging strong point: heterogeneity. Specifically, the city's ability to welcome, appreciate and support this mix of people, ethnic groups, cultures and traditions. And this is how the project came to be called “#MESCOLIAMOCI”. This is intended to be a collective and shared invitation to leave no one behind, to move in a direction of progress and innovation while fully enjoying the city of Turin. We are people and for this reason we are unique and indispensable. Let's merge, immerse ourselves and show how perfect “a combination of diversity” can be.
