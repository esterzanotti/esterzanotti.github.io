#PROJECT3

Ménage à trois

Fanzine realized for Politcnico of Turin-Design per la comunicazione

Sensuality, Sexuality and Design


In the shape of the triangle, over time, we have contained many meanings: divinity, the trinity, man's journey towards the divine, the Universal, the ascent from the multiple to the One. To the point of making it the symbol of a sentimental, loving and sexual relationship that includes the union of three individuals, three bodies, three minds in an exciting ménage à trois. It is with this vision that I want to take you to discover the projects that have particularly attracted my attention in the search for the meeting between sensuality, sexuality and design.
