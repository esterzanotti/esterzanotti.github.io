Imp(i)attare nel quotidiano

Group project made for Politecnico of Turin-Design di Scenario

Support and share

"Imp(i)attare nel quotidiano" is designed for university students realized to involve audience engagement and development of Banco ALimentare Piemonte.
The startign point of this concept is the stereotype that "italian people are the only one who talks about food while eating‚Äù, that's why the canteen is the most appropriate place to discuss about Food Banking.
The project consist of a collection of ten placemats, one for each month of the academic year, that face themes such as solidarity, connection with the territory and fight against food waste. Each of these themes is developed in an informative, interactive and awareness way.The collection is completed by a placemat to test knowledge learned during the previous months.
Audience engagement and development are achieved through the contents, in particular via an element that people can rip and take with them. That gives a direct user-product interaction.
Furthermore, this element becomes fundamental for establishing a connection with Banco Alimentare Piemonte thanks to the possibility of sharing terms and typical expressions of the Piedmontese dialect.
