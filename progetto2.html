#PROJECT2

Reda Rewoolution

Illustrations for the t-shirts of spring/summer collection

SS24: Connection and history

The graphics created for this project aim to illustrate the relation between the Biella Alps, where the outdoor clothing company is based, and the New Zealand mountain range of Mount Cook, where the Reda Rewoolution's merino sheep farms are located.
The connection between the mountains and the Biella wool tradition are taken up by a series of elements that characterize the graphics: the level curves, the coordinates, the altitudes, the ball of wool and the different skylines.

SS25: Kaitiakitanga


For the SS25 collection I decided to create a connection with the New Zealand culture, with which Reda has a beautiful relation based on some of the storical way to breed sheeps and take care of the environment. These illustrations represent the texture of nature combinated with some subject for the outdoor world.
