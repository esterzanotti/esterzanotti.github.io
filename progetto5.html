#PROJECT5

Il futuro fa 90, ma noi siamo 100mila

Group project designed for Politecnico of Turin-Digital Communication

Community and share

The project created for Arci Torino and Aria Spazi Reali aims to encourage the target of the service to create a community to talk about the issue of the future. For young people in Italy the future is the main source of stress, insecurity, uncertainty, anxiety, isolation and fear of failure. The precariousness that characterizes the latest generations is a problem that can cause serious psychological consequences. In a world where excellence is highlighted in record times, the work team wanted to remember the most important notion: "You are never alone!". The problem is common, shared, social and political; union is the only solution to walk towards change.
